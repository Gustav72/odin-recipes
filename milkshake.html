Chocolate Banana Milkshake

Yummy milkshake for those hot summer days!!!

Summary

Preperation Time: 35 minutes

Cook Time: 0 minutes

Total Time: 35 minutes

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 banana, frozen and chunked
  • 6 tablespoons powdered chocolate-flavored malt drink mix (such as OvaltineÂ®)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream

Steps

  1. Place the frozen banana chunks, powdered drink mix, milk, and vanilla ice cream into a blender, and blend until smooth and creamy. Pour into large glasses.
